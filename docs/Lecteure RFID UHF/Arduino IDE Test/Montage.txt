================================================================
        UHF RFID READER — CONNECTION NOTES
================================================================

STEP 1 : RFID Reader → MAX3232 (via DB9 RS232 cable)
----------------------------------------------------------------
The UHF RFID Reader communicates via RS232 protocol.
Since Arduino/ESP32 uses TTL (3.3V/5V), we need a 
MAX3232 converter to bridge the two.

  RFID Reader (DB9)       MAX3232
  -----------------       -------
  Pin 2 (RX)    ───────→  Pin 3 (TX)
  Pin 3 (TX)    ───────→  Pin 2 (RX)
  Pin 5 (GND)   ───────→  GND

NOTE : TX and RX must be crossed !
       TX of reader → RX of MAX3232
       RX of reader → TX of MAX3232

----------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2 : MAX3232 → Arduino UNO
----------------------------------------------------------------

  MAX3232         Arduino UNO
  -------         -----------
  RXD    ───────→ Pin 10 (SoftwareSerial RX)
  TXD    ←─────── Pin 11 (SoftwareSerial TX)
  GND    ───────→ GND
  VCC    ───────→ 5V

NOTE : Arduino UNO uses SoftwareSerial on pins 10 and 11
       because pins 0 and 1 are reserved for USB.
